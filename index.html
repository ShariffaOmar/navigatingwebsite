How to navigate during this time of difficults

In life one must encounter challenges in one way or the other.Challenges are meant to evaluate the level at which one may be able to solve that particular problem.During this difficult time of covid we must not loose hope and instead having faith that bad times will come to an end someday.There are ways in which young people in can do to deal with this time of pandemic.

Positivity

We should always think positive in every difficult situation.When you stay positive, you’re putting yourself in the best position possible to not only make it through those bad times, but become a better person in the process.You can remain positive and remind yourself that there really is a light at the end of the tunnel and that you’ll make it through.Even though how hard the situation is we must pick up the pieces and start moving forward.

Be Creative

There are times when you can’t do much to change your situation. You just have to deal with it. But there are other times when you can actively work to make the situation better.Find what you can do best and start working on it.Doing some arts is aslo a good way of revealing what you have in your heart and mind

Focus on What You Can Control

You should instead focus on the things that are within your control because that’s the only way you can make a change that’s actually going to help you. Make a list of everything you can control about the situation and divert all of your focus towards those things

Be Kind to Yourself

You need to care for yourself to survive tough times. Take a walk through the park, lift some weights, read an amazing book, watch some movies or even cooking your favourite meal. It doesn’t matter what you do, just do something that gets your mind and body engaged at a higher level.

Progressive structures inherited from colonial and western culture

There are some of the progressive structure as a result of colonialsm and western culture.

Education and literary skills

The African cultures that had no written script, quickly learnt how to get them as a result of European educators. In many parts of Africa where a written language existed, it was previously for a small elite as it had been in Europe in the middle ages. Besides the profiteering colonists came there was also a mixture of religious leaders bent on doing good. Some of them did much for education in Africa. Morover schools were constructed and hence Africans started schooling and were explored to many things.Even there was publication of different books to facilitate learning.Its a progressive culture since till now schools exists.The education skills has helped many Africans to gain knowledge in different fields and also helps in running the government.

Technology

Mechanisation grew exponentially during the colonial period. Africa, in many places endowed with natural climates that supported human life without as much support as would be needed in, for example, Europe, developed slower in terms of technology. The advent of colonisation brought such technologies to Africa.There was invention of different machines that makes work easier.The invention of power to support the running of different tasks that require power.Roads were constructed to allow easy movement of goods from different parts of Africa. Until now roads are beaing constructed for easy transportation of goods.Invention of telephones that made communication easier.There was also invention of different means of transport that are safer and faster e.g ships, aeroplane,busses,motorcycles e.t.c
